Remove near-duplicate messages to improve quality

Why deduplicate?

  • Increases variety in your dataset
  • Prevents bias from overrepresented content

How to deduplicate:

  1. Open the "Deduplication" section from the add-on menu
  2. Set similarity threshold
    Recommended setting: 0.9
    Higher = stricter matching (0.9 is a good balance)
  3. Click "Deduplicate Data" to create a clean version
Your original data will be preserved in a separate sheet
